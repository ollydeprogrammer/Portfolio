About Me
Olly de Boer

C#, Java & Python Developer | Aspiring Developer

Welcome to my website. If you’re a recruiter or hiring manager, this section is here to help you understand my journey.

I'll be honest, this isn’t the easiest thing to write. I had a challenging upbringing. My mum raised me as a single parent on disability benefits. We didn’t have much, but one thing I did have was a computer I built myself. That computer sparked a lifelong love of software, and technology.

At 18, I wasn’t sure what I wanted to do. Under pressure, I chose law and got really good at it (2:1). I then went on to study public policy and international affairs. These experiences helped me build skills I never imagined I’d have when I was younger.

But over time, particularly through my previous role in IT risk analysis, and through recent neurodiversity diagnosis', I realised my heart is in software, so I am now taking the steps to make this a reality. I started learning programming again in my spare time, beginning with the University of Helsinki’s Python MOOC. I was instantly hooked. Nothing had ever felt so intuitive or fulfilling. I’ve since picked up C# and Java, and I haven’t looked back.

I’m now focused on making this a career. I don’t mind how long it takes, I’m committed to the journey. All I’m hoping for is a chance from someone who believes in me as much as I do.

I believe you don’t need a degree to master something you’re passionate about. You need time, support, and motivation and I have all three. I’m dedicated, constantly learning, and more than willing to put in the work to improve.

This site is my way of showing that. Have a look through my GitHub and upcoming projects to see both my coding ability and my understanding of computer science principles.

Outside of tech, I’m into the outdoors, photography, running, and hiking. I’ve raised over £5,000 running marathons and love taking on new challenges.

Thanks for visiting. If you'd like to get in touch, my contact details are below and my CV is available for download.
